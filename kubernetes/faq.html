General

2015 Dates & Location

The Kubernetes 1.0 Launch will be held July 21, 2015 at the OREGON CONVENTION CENTER, Portland Ballroom in Portland, Oregon. An After Hours Party will be held later in the evening of July 21, 2015 at Rogue's Brewery's Green Dragon at 928 SE 9th Ave.

Stay Informed

To stay up to date with the latest information on sessions, speakers, and activities, be sure to visit the KUBERNETES 1.0 LAUNCH WEBSITE and follow us on Twitter. Follow and join in the Kubernetes 1.0 conversation via the official #Kubercon hashtag.

Content Formats

Throughout the day of the 21st, in-person attendees will be able to participate by attending the Keynote launch, Customer and Partner Showcase sessions, Q&A panels and more.

Live Stream & Recordings

If you can't make it to the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch event, you can still be a part of the experience by joining us via live stream, here. If you can't view it live, you will be able to watch YouTube recordings of all sessions, after the event, on our website.

Registration

Note: Attendance at the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch event requires a OSCON Expo Hall Plus pass. Free Expo Hall Plus passes are available for those not already registered for OSCON, via the discount code: KUBERNETESXPO. In order to gain access to the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch event, please be sure to REGISTER FOR OSCON.

Registration Details

  • This is a free event. Registration includes access to (1) the Kubernetes 1.0 Product Launch, which entails a full day of sessions and product demos, and (2) admission to the After Hours Party. We will be providing complimentary refreshments and light snacks throughout the day, and food and drinks at the After Hours Party.
  • You may register for both the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch and the After Hours Party or either seperately. Registration and a wristband (pick up during morning check-in) are required for the After Hours Party. Space is limited.
  • Onsite registration will be available as space allows. We recommend registering early as space is limited.
  • Transportation to and from the After Hours Party will be provided by Kubernetes. We will have buses picking up from the Oregon Convention Center beginning at 6:30 pm. The buses will be doing a continuous drop off and pick up loop until 11:30pm, between the event venue and the Oregon Convention Center. In addition to this route, starting at 10:00 pm, we will have a bus route transporting from the Brewery to the Marriott City Center at 550 SW Oak Street for those attendees staying in hotels on the West side of the river.
  • We suggest that those staying on the West side of the city utilize the Trimet MAX Lightrail for rides to the Oregon Convention Centre, where you will then be picked up via the Kubernetes bus transportation. Follow this link for directions to the Oregon Convention Center ACCESSIBLE via TriMet MAX Lightrail, Portland Streetcar, Uber, Taxi or walking directions, as many hotels are within walking distance.

Press Tickets

We have a limited number of press passes. If you are a journalist and have questions about Kubernetes, please email PRESS@GOOGLE.COM

Attendance Details

All presentations at the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch event will be in English. Registration is required for attendance at all sessions and events for the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch. Additionally, the event is on a first-come, first-served basis.

Pro Tips

If you're joining us in person at the Oregon Convention Center, here are some useful tips to navigate your way to the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch:

  • Badge Pick-up: Admittance to the event requires an OSCON badge, which can be picked up on Monday, July 20 between 7:00 AM and 7:00 PM, and on Tuesday, July 21 between 7:30 AM and 6:00 PM. As seating is limited, we recommend picking up badges early and planning accordingly.
  • Wristbands: Wristbands are required for entrance to the After Hours Party. Each person who has registered for the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch After Hours Party will receive a wristband during morning check-in, at the entrance to the Launch, for access to the After Hours party. Attendees must have the wristband on at all times.
  • Meals: Attendees are invited to join us for light snacks and refreshments throughout the day, and complimentary drinks and dinner at the After Hours Party.
  • After Hours Party: This is a party you don't want to miss! Attendees are invited to enjoy food, drinks, music, games and more throughout the evening of July 21 at Rogue Brewery's Green Dragon. The wristband is required for entrance. Space is limited so be sure to register early.

After Hours Party

All registered attendees are invited to the After Hours party. Kubernetes wristbands and registration are required for entry. Please join us for food, drinks, music, games, and more. The After Hours Party is a 21 and over event.

Event Attire

This is a casual, developers event. Please dress in a way that is comfortable to you. There is no enforced dress code.

Anti-harassment Policy

Kubernetes by Google is dedicated to providing a harassment-free event experience for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, gender identity, age, race, national identity, or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of event participants in any form.

Code of Conduct Policy

Kubernetes events are working conferences intended for professional networking and collaboration in the Kubernetes community. Attendees are expected to behave according to professional standards and in accordance with their employer's policies on appropriate workplace behavior.

While at Kubernetes events or related social networking opportunities, attendees should not engage in discriminatory or offensive speech or actions regarding gender, sexuality, race, or religion. Speakers should be especially aware of these concerns.

The Kubernetes team does not condone any statements by speakers contrary to these standards. The Kubernetes team reserves the right to deny entrance and/or eject from an event (without refund) any individual found to be engaging in discriminatory or offensive speech or actions.

Please bring any concerns to to the immediate attention of Kubernetes event staff, or contact Brendan Burns at brendan.d.burns(at)gmail(dot)com.

Smoking

Smoking is not permitted anywhere inside the Oregon Convention Center Center. Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor smoking areas.

No Soliciting

No solicitation or selling of items or services is allowed at the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch event. Any attendee conducting these activities may be removed from the conference.
