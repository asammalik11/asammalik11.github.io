After Hours Party – Sponsored by Intel

After the Keynote and Customer and Partner Showcase, registered attendees are invited to kick back and raise a pint in celebration. The free After Hours Party sponsored by Intel will be held at an undisclosed Portland hotspot and will give every attendee a true taste of Portland – Google & Intel style.

Don't forget – a Kubernetes wristband is required to attend this epic party, and space is limited. Be sure to get yours at the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch registration desk that morning, located just outside of the Portland Ballroom, prior to the keynote.
