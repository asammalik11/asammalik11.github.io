Come celebrate the launch of Kubernetes 1.0 at an epic After Hours Party, sponsored by Intel. Join us at the Rogue Brewery's Green Dragon - a bar dedicated to the education & appreciation of craft beer.

Register Today

It will be a memorable evening filled with shenanigans including (but not limited to): Giant Jenga, Cornhole and Squid Tossing...it'll be as funky and fun as it sounds!

21 and over only

No Portland event would be complete without an appearance by the Portland Unipiper, Voodoo Doughnuts, and two live outdoor performances by local performing artists Supercrow and Kevin Lee Florence.

Registration required! Space is limited.

Free Transportation

We will have a number of chartered buses circulating between the Rogue Brewery's Green Dragon and the Oregon Convention Centre beginning at 6:30 PM and running until 11:30 PM. At 10:00 PM, buses will also begin dropping guests off at the Marriott Courtyard (550 SW Oak Street) for those staying on the west side of the city. Look for the Kubernetes 1.0 Launch signs. Buses will be – running back and forth every 30 minutes so be sure to time your entrance!
